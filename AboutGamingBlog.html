What is Woopi's Gaming Blog?

Woopi's Gaming Blog is a blog specifically made for gamers and enthusists all over the world to get the reviews on the latest video games releases. Here at Woopi's Gaming Blog we try our hardest to have a none bias review on all sorts of titles such as Legend of Zelda, Pokemon, Ubisoft, etc.

Who is Woopi and why does he have a Gaming blog?

Woopi: "I am a gamer from the United States who has a passion for gaming and video game culture. One day while I was tying to find a good review on a game I was interested in, I noticed there are very few non-bias reviews on the internet. At the time I was learning how to use html and css and decided that I would make a site for Gamers with reliable game reviews. I am happy to be here to provide reviews for you. enjoy!"
